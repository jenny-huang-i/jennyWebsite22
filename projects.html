Othello AI

Utilizing heuristic search and a tailored evaluation function, the AI executes the most desirable move based on the current game board.

  • With the limitation of three seconds of CPU time, the program evaluted the current board, considered all avalible moves for a specified depth, and executed a play based on minimuns and maximums.

    • Maze Generator + Solver

    Generated random perfect mazes and solved them.

  • Utilizing depth first traversal, a perfect maze (one where any two cells are connected by one unique path) was created and solved independent of each other.
  • Given a maze with an arbitrary size, a solution was found using a similar method to above.

    • Spell Checker

    Identified misspelled words and generated suggestions.

  • Utilized three data structures for storage and lookup of words. (AVL Tree, HashMap, and Skip List).
  • Implemented five different techniques to recognize misspelled words.

    • Bomb Sweeper
